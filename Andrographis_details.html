Andrographis

Scientific Name: Andrographis paniculata

Medicinal Use: Andrographis is renowned for its immune-boosting and anti-inflammatory properties. It is commonly used to treat respiratory infections, fever, and digestive disorders.

Advantages

  • Boosts the immune system and helps in fighting infections.
  • Has anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.
  • Supports liver health and detoxification.
  • Effective in treating respiratory issues and fever.

Disadvantages

  • May cause gastrointestinal discomfort or nausea in some individuals.
  • Can interact with certain medications, including blood thinners.
  • Not recommended for pregnant or breastfeeding women without medical advice.

Medicinal Uses

  • Used to enhance immune function and combat infections.
  • Helps reduce inflammation and supports liver health.
  • Effective in managing symptoms of fever and respiratory conditions.
  • May aid in digestion and treat gastrointestinal disorders.
